About Lokmangal Group

Established over 50 years ago, Lokmangal Group of Institutions has grown into one of Maharashtra's most respected educational networks. With 10+ colleges spanning Engineering, Arts, Science, Commerce, and Professional studies, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential. Backed by world-class infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we bridge the gap between education and employability.
