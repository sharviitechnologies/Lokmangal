Our Legacy

A Story Rooted in
Rural Transformation

The Lokmangal Group of Institutions was founded in 2003 under the visionary leadership of Hon. Shri. Subhashrao Deshmukh, with the profound conviction that quality education must reach every corner of rural Maharashtra — not merely the privileged few in urban centres.

Starting with the flagship Lokmangal Agriculture College in Wadala (Nanded), the group has grown into a thriving ecosystem of eight specialised institutions spanning agriculture, biotechnology, pharmacy, nursing, science, business management, ITI, and junior college education.

Beyond classrooms, Lokmangal has established more than 25 live projects — from Biodiversity Parks and Bio-Gas Plants to Community Radio and a Water Bank — embedding sustainability and entrepreneurship into its DNA. These initiatives are not merely add-ons; they are laboratories where students learn by doing, producing, and impacting.

Today, Lokmangal stands as a beacon for rural youth, proving that ambition and opportunity need not be confined to cities. Its campuses pulse with curiosity, innovation, and a deep sense of social responsibility.

Purpose & Direction

Vision & Mission

Every institution we build is guided by a clear compass — empowering the rural youth of India through holistic, practice-based education.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognised centre of agricultural and multidisciplinary education, producing skilled, ethical, and innovative professionals who uplift rural communities and contribute meaningfully to a sustainable India.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and industry-relevant education; to foster research and innovation; and to nurture graduates who are socially conscious, technically proficient, and capable of transforming Indian agriculture and allied sectors.

Community Impact

We are committed to the holistic development of the communities surrounding our campuses — through extension activities, farmer training, soil testing services, and community healthcare outreach that translate education into real-world impact.

Innovation & Research

Through our network of live projects, tissue culture labs, bio-fertiliser units, and a community radio station, we embed experiential research into everyday learning — making innovation a habit, not a milestone.

Academic Excellence

Affiliated with leading universities and governed by statutory bodies, all Lokmangal institutions maintain rigorous academic standards — combining structured curricula with the flexibility to innovate, experiment, and grow.

Student Welfare

From scholarships and hostel facilities to career guidance and placement support, Lokmangal wraps every student in a safety net of resources — so that ambition is never limited by circumstance.

Founder's Message

A Dream Born from
the Fields of Maharashtra

Education is the most powerful instrument we can give to a child in a village. When I founded Lokmangal, my dream was simple yet vast: to ensure that the son of a farmer in Nanded has access to the same quality of education as anyone in Pune or Mumbai. Twenty years on, that dream is alive — not just in our campuses, but in the careers, confidence, and communities our graduates have built across Maharashtra and beyond.

Our name, Lokmangal, means "welfare of the people" — and every decision we make, every institution we establish, every project we launch, is anchored in that promise. We do not just train professionals; we shape citizens who feel a deep duty to give back to the soil and the society that nurtured them.

Hon. Shri. Subhashrao Deshmukh Founder & Chairman — Lokmangal Group of Institutions
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Integrity

Transparency, honesty, and ethical conduct underpin everything we do — from academic evaluation to institutional governance.

02

Innovation

We champion creative thinking and encourage students to question, experiment, and develop solutions for real-world agricultural and social challenges.

03

Inclusivity

Quality education is a right, not a privilege. We actively reach students from underprivileged and rural backgrounds through accessible fee structures and scholarships.

04

Excellence

We set and pursue high standards across every discipline — in teaching, research, campus facilities, and student outcomes — refusing to be ordinary.

05

Social Responsibility

Our campuses are deeply embedded in local communities. We give back through extension services, farmer training, healthcare camps, and environmental conservation.

06

Sustainability

With projects like rain water harvesting, bio-gas plants, and vermi-composting, we model sustainable living — teaching the next generation to be stewards of the earth.
